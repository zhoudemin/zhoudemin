## 🚀 Products

### 🧾 ScanCove

**Receipts → Organized expense reports**

ScanCove turns receipt photos into structured expense records and professional expense reports.

It is designed for freelancers, small businesses, project workers, and anyone who needs a faster way to organize receipts and prepare reimbursements.

#### Main features

- Scan receipts with the camera
- Import receipt images from Photos
- Extract merchant, date, amount, tax, and category
- Review and edit recognized receipt information
- Organize receipts by project or month
- Create professional expense reports
- Export reports as PDF or Excel
- Synchronize data through the user's own iCloud
- No account required
- Privacy-first design

🌐 [Visit ScanCove](https://scancove.app)

> ScanCove is proprietary software. Its source code is not publicly available.

### 🛠️ QuoteCove

**Speak the job. Send the quote before you leave.**

QuoteCove helps solo home-service professionals create estimates and invoices without spending unnecessary time on paperwork.

Users can describe a job in a few spoken sentences, generate a structured estimate based on their own rates, and convert the estimate into an invoice when the work is complete.

#### Built for

- Handymen
- Plumbers
- Electricians
- Painters
- HVAC technicians
- Cleaners
- Landscapers
- Repair professionals
- Independent contractors
- Solo home-service businesses

#### Main features

- Turn spoken job details into a professional estimate
- Create estimates from photos and written descriptions
- Price work using the user's own rates
- Maintain a personal price book
- Convert estimates into invoices
- Track draft, sent, unpaid, and paid documents
- Reuse customer and service information
- Reduce repetitive typing and paperwork

🌐 [Visit QuoteCove](https://quotecove.app)

> QuoteCove is proprietary software. Its source code is not publicly available.

### 🎼 LingxiScore

**AI-assisted numbered musical notation and score editing**

LingxiScore is a music notation tool focused on numbered musical notation, score recognition, choir arrangements, and efficient music editing.

中文名称：**灵溪制谱**

The project is designed to make it easier to convert photographed or scanned music into editable numbered notation, especially for choir, hymn, and multi-voice arrangements.

#### Main features

- Image-to-score recognition
- AI-assisted music transcription
- Numbered musical notation editing
- Multi-voice score support
- Key signatures and accidentals
- Rhythm and duration notation
- Lyrics and score alignment
- Choir and hymn arrangement workflows
- Score preview and correction
- Print-ready score generation
- Export for sharing and rehearsal

> LingxiScore is a private project. Its source code is not publicly available.

## 🌱 Development Philosophy

I prefer building focused products instead of large, feature-heavy platforms.

My products aim to be:

- Easy to understand
- Fast to use
- Focused on one clear problem
- Respectful of user privacy
- Useful without unnecessary accounts
- Designed for real work instead of demonstrations
- Simple on the surface, reliable underneath

I believe good software should reduce friction rather than create more of it.

---

## 🔐 Privacy Principles

Privacy is an important part of my product design.

Whenever possible, my apps are designed around these principles:

- No unnecessary user accounts
- No unnecessary personal-data collection
- No advertising tracking
- Local processing where practical
- User-controlled iCloud synchronization
- Clear explanations of how data is used
- Minimal dependence on external services
